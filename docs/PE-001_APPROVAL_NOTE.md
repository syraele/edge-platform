# PE-001 Approval Note

Version: 1.0

Status: Prepared

Phase: Platform Evolution

---

# Purpose

This note summarizes the approval posture of PE-001.

It is intended to support a formal review decision by making clear what is being proposed, what is being protected, and what conditions must be satisfied before implementation proceeds.

---

# Proposal Summary

PE-001 proposes a controlled plugin extension mechanism for EDGE_ENGINE.

The proposal is architectural rather than product-oriented. Its purpose is to expand the platform’s extensibility without weakening the Core architecture, the research mission, or the scientific integrity of the system.

---

# What Must Be Protected

The following principles must remain intact:

- Foundation v2 must remain the architectural baseline.
- The Domain Model must remain unchanged.
- The Core must remain protected from uncontrolled modification.
- Research integrity, reproducibility, and traceability must remain central.

---

# Approval Conditions

PE-001 should be approved only if the following conditions are satisfied:

- the plugin architecture is explicit and coherent;
- the extension contract is clear;
- the lifecycle and boundaries are documented;
- the specification is sufficiently explicit to guide future implementation safely;
- the milestone remains aligned with the Platform Principles and the research mission.

---

# Review Outcome

If these conditions are satisfied, PE-001 is ready to be treated as an approved architectural milestone for the next implementation stage.

If they are not satisfied, the milestone should remain in the Specification phase until the gaps are resolved.
